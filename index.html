About Capybara Testing

Empowering anyone to become a confident software creator.

Our Mission

Capybara Testing introduces complete beginners to programming by removing technical intimidation, complex setup hurdles, and confusing developer jargon.

Our Vision

We envision a world where programming literacy is accessible, ADA-inclusive, calm, and collaborative for everyone—regardless of prior technical experience.

Why Beginners Choose Us

We replace steep learning curves with gentle step-by-step curriculum, immediate peer mentorship, and structured project building that honors your learning pace.
